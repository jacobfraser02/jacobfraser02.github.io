About Me

Hi, I’m Jacob — a Deaf community engagement worker and advocate passionate about making sure Deaf people feel seen, heard, and supported. I work at SignHealth, leading outreach and events to raise awareness about domestic abuse in the Deaf community. I run workshops, create BSL-accessible content, and help build safe spaces where Deaf survivors know their rights and can get the support they need.

Before this, I worked in Deaf social care, offering one-to-one support that helped people live more independently. I’m all about breaking down barriers, having honest conversations, and making sure Deaf voices are always at the centre.

I believe real change happens when we lead with access, empathy, and community.
